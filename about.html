about me

Hello! My name is Krystle Vollmer and I am the person behind Krystlize. I am a graphic designer located in Lincoln, Nebraska. I enjoy learning new skills and trying new ways to design. My passion for design grows exponentially everyday. When not obsessing over design, I enjoy finding new music, researching the world, and I am a video game aficionado.

My interest in design started at a young age, but my decision to become a designer started while I was in another profession: digital printing. I was a digital press operator for six years and loved my job, but I wanted to move up into design. Years of working closely with the designers and watching them work made me yearn to join them. I could not be happier with my choice!

Like my branding? The logo was created by my good friend (who is also a designer) Will Spickelmier!
