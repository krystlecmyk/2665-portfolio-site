dot soda

Package Design Concept

Dot is a small soda company that has been making soda for 30 years and have only been selling their product locally. They are releasing a new line of different flavored soda beverages. Most Americans drink soda, but we targeted teenagers and adults who have money to spend on soft drinks. These people are 15 – 40 years old, and are already soda drinkers. They are also creative in their flavors so the design needed to be creative and have a fun tone.
