Logo / Style Guide Concept

Odd Duck is an oddities shop that sells macabre objects and antiques. They have opened many stores nation wide along with launching a website and were in need of a logo and consistent use of branding. They wanted a playful, but dark perception of their company. The style guide goes through usage of type, logo, and pictures for promoting the business.
