aperture science

Aperture Laboratories is a premiere science testing facility that collects data based off of their various inventions. They test many things including potentially hazardous/dangerous products. This is a conceptual 2014 annual report with inforgaphics about the past year's test results. This booklet would come as a package with a special-edition release of Portal and Portal 2 game bundle. The target audience for this piece are gamers who are fans of the Portal games and the Half-life 2 game.
