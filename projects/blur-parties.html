blur parties

Blur Parties is a tailgate and event production company. They start from the ground up for events and consider every detail to ensure the best experience for their clients. Their Husker tailgate is currently their best event and are hoping to make it more successful. The target audience is between the ages 21 to 45 and will have time to spare on a day long event. They have a passion for Husker tailgating. Individuals will also need to have spare income to spend on social activities. Blur has wanted to find a new, fun, playful, and creative ad series to promote their company. The name blur comes from the idea of blurring the lines between corporate and party animals. Playing off of the name blur, I designed a series of ads with the tagline We focus on YOU!
