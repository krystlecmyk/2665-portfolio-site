Ad Campaign

Blur Parties is a tailgate and event production company that considers every detail to ensure the best experience for their clients. Their Husker tailgate is currently their most successful event due to passionate fans. Blur has wanted to find a new, fun, playful, and creative ad series to promote their company. The name blur comes from the idea of blurring the lines between corporate and party animals. Playing off of the name blur I created their new tagline, "We focus on YOU!"
