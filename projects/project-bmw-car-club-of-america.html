BMW Car Club of America

The BMW Car Club of America is a well established car club with nearly 75,000 members currently. They are recognized by BMW and do a lot with retailers, mechanics, and specialists to have a close relationship with their idol. Target audience 20 to 40 year-old BMW owners with spare money and time from club gatherings. These are conceptual promotional pieces for new and exsisting members including: membership brochure with mail-in application, poster, and keychain.
