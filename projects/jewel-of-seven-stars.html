jewel of seven stars

Novel Design Concept

The Jewel of Seven Stars is a mummy horror novel written by the famous Bram Stoker who is known for his famous novel Dracula. Workman Publishing would like to re-release the lesser known novel. Target audience of 20 – 30 year-old horror novel fans with discretionary income. This is a concept book design including cover, page layout, and chapter graphics that correlate to the story.
